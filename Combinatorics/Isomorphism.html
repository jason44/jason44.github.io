General tips for showing an isomorphism:
  • If the complements are simpler, show isomorphism of complements
  • Show isomorphism of subgraphs to reduce complexity
  • If there is nothing too obvious, start by mapping vertices that must be related (or choose one random one if there is no standout relation), then show the same for its neighbors and so on recursively (naive isomorphism algorithm).
