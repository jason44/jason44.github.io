Normal Force

The normal force is the component of a contact force (the force that results when two objects make contact) that is perpendicular to the surface that the object contacts.

Here, the normal force represents the force applied by the ground against the mass that prevents the mass from sinking through the ground.

Friction

Friction is the force applied by a surface, fluid layers, or materials sliding against each other.

Static Friction

Friction is applied onto a mass that contacts a surface (and vice-versa). The force on the mass resists its motion that is, the force of friction is opposite the force of motion.
