Energy for Linear One-Dimensional Systems

The analysis of energy is much simpler when the object in question is constrained into moving within a single dimension. The simplest one dimension system is one where the object moves linearly in a single direction . But more complex systems such as a roller coaster on a curving track can also be measured with a single parameter (such as the distance of the roller coaster along its track).
