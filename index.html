Learn and Practice

Learn mathematics from the beginning in a unique way. I start with propositional logic and sets because we describe much of mathematics with them. Next, I cover topics from algebra which can be skipped if the reader feels that it is too much review. The next section covers geometry. The final section of the book covers a wide variety of topics from determinants to convolutions. Some sections contain problems for the reader to work out with answers located at the back of the book.
